What Invisalign® Can Treat?

Invisalign® clear aligners can treat nearly all common teeth-straightening issues, from simple to complex.

Overly Crowded Teeth

When there isn't enough space for your teeth to fit normally in the jaws.

Underbite

When one or more lower teeth protrude in front of your upper teeth.

Open Bite

When both upper and lower teeth are forced outwards so that they don't touch each other.

Overbite

When one or more of your upper teeth protrude over your lower teeth.

Gaps in Teeth

Gaps can cause teeth to shift into the empty spaces and become loose.

Crossbite

When one or more of the upper teeth bite on the inside of the lower teeth.

Frequently Asked Questions

How Much Does Invisalign® Cost?

The cost of Invisalign® can vary from patient to patient, but generally, treatment can cost between £3,000 - £5000 depending on the complexity of the case. However, if only minor tooth movement is required, prices could start from £1,495. With our finance options, treatment can cost as little as £29 per Month!
How Does Invisalign® Work?

Invisalign® works by using a series of clear, plastic, made-for-you aligners that are changed out every few weeks to quickly and effectively guide the straightening of your teeth. Invisalign® offers you a minimally invasive solution to the teeth straightening process, with absolutely minimal disruption to your day to day life as possible.

How Long Does Invisalign® Treatment Take?

Depending on the complexity of your case, average treatment time can be between 6 - 18 months. To find out how long your treatment would take, we offer a free Smile Assessment.
Who Can Have Invisalign®?

Most conditions are treatable with Invisalign®. In some extreme cases Invisalign® may not be suitable for you. Book a free Smile Assessment with a member of our team today to find out how Invisalign® could improve your smile.
